The H2 Story

We didn't invent the hat. We rebuilt it.

H2 began on a wet Tuesday off the Carolina coast, watching three captains complain about the same thing: every cap fails in the spray. Sun-bleached. Salt-warped. Soaked.

Two years of prototyping, six fabric mills, and forty-one rejected samples later — H2 is what a performance hat should have been all along. Quietly engineered. Visibly refined.
